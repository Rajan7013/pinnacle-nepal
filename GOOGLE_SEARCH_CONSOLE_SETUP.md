# Google Search Console Setup Guide
## For Pinnacle Nepal

## What is Google Search Console?

Google Search Console is a free tool that helps you:
- Monitor your website's presence in Google Search
- See which keywords people use to find your site
- Submit your sitemap for faster indexing
- Fix SEO issues and errors
- Track your search rankings

---

## Step-by-Step Setup

### Step 1: Go to Google Search Console

1. **Open:** https://search.google.com/search-console
2. **Sign in** with your Google account (use rajanprasaila@gmail.com or vivek@pinnaclenepal.org)

### Step 2: Add Your Property

1. **Click "Add Property"**
2. **Choose "URL prefix"**
3. **Enter:** `https://www.pinnaclenepal.org`
4. **Click "Continue"**

### Step 3: Verify Ownership (DNS Method - Recommended)

**Google will show you a TXT record to add to your DNS.**

1. **Copy the TXT record** (looks like: `google-site-verification=ABC123XYZ...`)
2. **Go to Cloudflare Dashboard:** https://dash.cloudflare.com
3. **Select your domain:** pinnaclenepal.org
4. **Click "DNS"**
5. **Click "Add record"**
6. **Configure:**
   - **Type:** TXT
   - **Name:** @ (or leave blank)
   - **Content:** Paste the verification code from Google
   - **TTL:** Auto
7. **Click "Save"**
8. **Go back to Google Search Console**
9. **Click "Verify"**

**Verification usually takes a few minutes. If it fails, wait 10-15 minutes and try again.**

---

### Step 4: Submit Your Sitemap

Once verified:

1. **In Google Search Console**, go to **"Sitemaps"** (left sidebar)
2. **Enter:** `sitemap.xml`
3. **Click "Submit"**

**Google will start crawling your website!**

---

### Step 5: Monitor Your Performance

After 2-3 days, you'll start seeing data:

1. **Performance:** See which keywords bring traffic
2. **Coverage:** Check if all pages are indexed
3. **Enhancements:** Fix any SEO issues
